YOUR DECISIONS MATTER!

There are many little changes you can make to how you live your life to take a stand against the destruction of our world. Start with the little things you purcahse for your diet,everyday supplies and clothing!For instance switch from cow's milk to non-dairy milks like soy or oat milk, etc. which the production of causes a lot of greenhouse gas emissions, water degradation, and loss of natural habitats. Support your local farmers' markets and small businesses instead of big corporations or brands that are contributing to the harm being done to the environment. Most importantly is to shop condiderably; think again when making a purchase and keep questions like:"Do I need this?","If the packaging is made out of plastic,are there any alternitive?","Do you stand by the values of the campany that you are purchasing from?","Cound you possibly get the product secondhanded?" etc.
